Privacy Policy

Simple & clear

What we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible.

  • User content you provide: selected movie/series choice, optional personal photo.
  • App diagnostics (optional): crash logs / performance data to improve stability.

How we use it

  • To generate SkinArt based on your selected options.
  • To provide support and troubleshoot issues if you contact us.
  • To improve app reliability (crashes, bugs, performance).

Storage & retention

  • Generated results may be stored locally on your device if you save/favorite them.
  • If you upload a photo for generation, it is used for processing and not intended to be kept longer than necessary.

Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may use third-party services for app functionality (e.g., hosting, analytics) depending on your build.

Children

Movie SkinArt is not intended for children under 13.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to provide a personal photo.
  • You can delete saved/favorited items in the app.
  • Contact us to request help with data questions.
